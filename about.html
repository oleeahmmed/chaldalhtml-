About Chaldal

Your trusted partner for fresh groceries and daily essentials

Our Story

Founded in 2013, Chaldal started with a simple mission: to make grocery shopping convenient and accessible for everyone in Bangladesh. What began as a small startup has grown into the country's leading online grocery platform.

We believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, quality products without the hassle of traditional shopping. Our platform connects customers with local suppliers and farmers, ensuring freshness while supporting local communities.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across major cities in Bangladesh, delivering everything from fresh vegetables to household essentials right to their doorstep.

Our Mission

To revolutionize the way people shop for groceries by providing a seamless, reliable, and efficient online platform that delivers fresh, quality products to customers' doorsteps while supporting local communities and promoting sustainable practices.

Our Values

Freshness

We guarantee the freshness of all our products with direct sourcing from farms and trusted suppliers.

Reliability

Timely delivery and consistent service quality are at the heart of everything we do.

Community

We support local farmers and suppliers, contributing to the growth of our community.
